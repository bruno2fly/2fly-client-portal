Frequently asked questions

How does the client portal work?
+
Each client gets their own branded portal where they can approve content, submit requests, check their calendar, and see monthly progress reports. They only see their own content — nothing from your other clients. You can customize it with your agency's branding.
Can my designers use it?
+
Yes! Designers get their own dashboard with task queues, brief details, and a place to upload final art directly. When they submit work, it goes straight to the approval flow — no more downloading files and re-uploading them somewhere else.
Does it post directly to Instagram?
+
Yes. Connect your Meta Business accounts and you can schedule and publish posts directly to Instagram and Facebook from the content calendar. No need for third-party scheduling tools.
Is there a free trial?
+
We offer a free demo with a personal walkthrough of the platform. During early access, you also get a 14-day money-back guarantee — if it's not for you, we'll refund you completely.
Can I import existing content?
+
Yes. You can import assets from Google Drive, upload files directly, or create content from scratch in the platform. We also help with migration during your onboarding call.
How is my data protected?
+
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use enterprise-grade hosting with 99.9% uptime. Your client data is isolated and never shared. We're GDPR compliant and take data security seriously.
