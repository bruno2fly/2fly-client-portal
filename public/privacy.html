2FlyFlow Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

1. Introduction

2FlyFlow ("we", "our", "us") operates the 2FlyFlow platform at 2flyflow.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including your name, email address, business name, and login credentials when you create an account. When you connect social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram), we receive access tokens and page/account information necessary to publish content on your behalf.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to provide and improve our services, including managing client portals, scheduling and publishing social media content to connected Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts, managing content approval workflows, and communicating with you about your account.

4. Facebook and Instagram Data

When you connect Facebook or Instagram through our platform, we access your Facebook Pages and Instagram Business accounts solely to publish content you have approved and scheduled. We store access tokens securely and do not share this data with third parties. You can disconnect your social accounts at any time from the Scheduled Posts tab in your dashboard.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information and access tokens. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you disconnect a social media account, we delete the associated access tokens. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. You can disconnect social media integrations at any time. You may request a copy of your data or ask us to delete your account by contacting us at the email below.

8. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and maintain your session. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at 2flydigitalmarketing@gmail.com.
